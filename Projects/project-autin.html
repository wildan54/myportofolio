AUTIN – Autism Independence Monitoring System

Python (Flask) Bootstrap

Overview

AUTIN is a web-based system designed to monitor the independence level of children with autism through video-based activity analysis. Users upload videos of daily activities such as eating and drinking, and the system analyzes behavior patterns to determine independence levels.
